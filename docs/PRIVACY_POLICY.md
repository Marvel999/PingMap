# Privacy Policy — PingMap (“WiFi Advisor”)

**Last updated:** March 9, 2026  

**App:** PingMap (package: `com.marvel999.pingmap`)  
**Developer / data contact:** Manish Singh — **Manishniec007@gmail.com**

This policy describes how PingMap handles information when you use the app. PingMap is a **network utility** that helps you understand Wi‑Fi around you, run on-device checks, and optionally store history **on your device**.

By using PingMap, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

---

## 1. Summary

- **Wi‑Fi scanning and scoring run on your device.** We do not operate our own cloud backend for core scan results.
- **Sensitive data** (location, Wi‑Fi identifiers, network details) is used **to provide app features** as described below.
- **Most data stays on your phone** in local storage (database and settings), unless you use features that **contact the internet** (for example, a speed test).
- We **do not sell your personal information**.

---

## 2. Information the app may access or process

### 2.1 Location (approximate / precise)

- **Why:** On Android, **location permission is required** for apps to access Wi‑Fi scan results (system requirement on supported versions).  
- **How it is used:** To discover nearby Wi‑Fi networks, show connection status, scoring, and related tools **inside the app**.  
- **We do not use your location to build an advertising profile** in PingMap.

### 2.2 Wi‑Fi and network data

The app may read or derive:

- Wi‑Fi network names (**SSIDs**), identifiers (**BSSIDs**), signal strength, band/channel, security type, and similar scan metadata.  
- **Current connection** information (for example, whether you are connected and basic link details shown in the UI).

This data is used **to display information and scores in the app** and, if you enable it, **to save scan history locally**.

### 2.3 Data stored locally on your device

Depending on how you use PingMap and your settings, the app may store **locally** (for example, using on-device database and preferences):

- **Scan history** (if “Save scan history” or equivalent is enabled): past scan sessions and summarized network results.  
- **Speed test results** (timestamps, measured speeds), if you use the speed test feature.  
- **Device discovery / network tool outputs** you generate in the app (for example, devices or measurements you choose to keep in the app).  
- **App settings** (for example: auto-scan, notifications preferences, expert details, language, background monitoring toggle, onboarding completion).

**Uninstalling the app** removes app data from your device unless Android backup/restore copies it according to your device settings.

### 2.4 Internet and speed tests

- If you run a **speed test**, the app may connect to a **speed test server** (URL configurable in app settings or defaults) **over the internet** to measure performance.  
- That server may see **standard connection metadata** needed to perform the test (for example, your IP address as seen by the server, timing, and volume of test traffic), **as with any speed test on the internet**.

PingMap does not need to collect your name, email, or account for basic use of the app.

### 2.5 Notifications (optional)

- If you enable features that use **notifications** (for example, alerts related to network safety or background monitoring), the system may show notifications **on your device**.  
- **Notification permission** (Android 13+) is only used for these in-app purposes, not for unrelated marketing from us via this app.

### 2.6 Background monitoring (optional)

- If you turn on **background monitoring**, the app may run **limited background work** (for example, periodic checks or a foreground service as implemented) **to keep you informed about connectivity**, subject to Android rules and your settings.  
- This processing is intended **for the app’s network-monitoring features**, not for advertising.

## 3. How we use this information

We use the information above only to:

- Provide PingMap’s features (Wi‑Fi advisor, scanner, tools, history, speed test, optional monitoring).  
- Remember your preferences.  
- Improve stability and security of the app (for example, handling errors on device).

---

## 4. Sharing of information

We **do not sell** your personal information.

We may share information **only** when:

- **You cause data to leave the device** through a feature (for example, contacting a **third-party speed test server**). That provider processes data under **their own policies**.  
- **Required by law** or to protect rights and safety.

There is **no PingMap account server** described in this policy for routine syncing of your scans; primary processing is **on-device**.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate for a local-first mobile app (secure storage APIs, minimal data collection). **No method is 100% secure.** Protect your device with a lock screen and keep the OS updated.

## 7. Your choices and controls

- **Permissions:** You can **deny** location or notification permissions; some features (Wi‑Fi scanning, notifications) **will not work** without them, by Android design.  
- **Settings:** Disable **scan history**, **auto speed test**, **background monitoring**, or other toggles in **Settings** inside the app where available.  
- **Deletion:** **Uninstall** PingMap to remove app data from your device (subject to device backup rules).
